## 2. The pre-sell offer — "Founding Members"

Don't wait for the platform. Sell the outcome now at a founder price.

- **Founding Member**: first 10 customers, **50% off for life** (Starter €49→€25,
  Pro €149→€75), onboarded personally by you. Scarcity is real: cap at 10.
- **What they buy:** the JARVIS outcome (leads found + outreach drafted for
  THEIR business), delivered by you as a service *now*, migrating into the
  self-serve dashboard when Phase 3–4 ship.
- **Two ways to collect commitment:**
  1. **Paid pre-order** via Stripe Checkout (the €25/€75 plan, live once Phase 4
     lands — days away).
  2. **LOI / deposit** before that: a Stripe Payment Link for a refundable €50
     "founding deposit" that locks the 50%-for-life rate. (Payment Links need
     no code — you create one in the Stripe dashboard today.)

## 3. The 30-day pre-sell sprint

**Week 1 — Sharpen & seed**
- Finish the landing redesign + add a "Founding Members — 10 spots" section
  with the offer and a waitlist→deposit CTA.
- CALEB writes the offer copy; PIXEL reviews the page.
- Warm the existing **waitlist**: one honest email — "we're opening 10 founding
  spots, you're first."

**Week 2 — Run the machine on real niches**
- Pick 2 niches you can serve well (e.g. Warsaw dental clinics, real-estate
  agencies — you already have 41 scored leads).
- ORACLE tops up contacts → ATLAS drafts → **you approve** → HERALD sends.
- Target: 40 personalized first-touches sent. Reply-watch on.

**Week 3 — Convert conversations**
- Every reply → a 15-min call (you close). Offer Founding Member on the call.
- followup agent drafts second-touches for non-replies.
- Publish 3 pieces of proof (a PIXEL teardown of a real site, a before/after,
  a short "how NOXIOAI found 12 leads in one run" post — content-engine skill).

**Week 4 — Close & lock**
- Convert calls → deposits/pre-orders via Stripe.
- **Target: 3–5 Founding Members committed** (paid deposit or subscription).
  Even 3 × €25 is proof the funnel converts — the real win is the *validated
  motion*, not the €75.

## 4. Targets (be honest, not vanity)

| Metric | 30-day target | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| First-touches sent | 40 | Enough to learn what converts |
